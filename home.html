ABOUT US

Stallion Karting is a team of 30+ students from different disciplines in SKNCOE, Pune. We are a collective of passionate individuals who are dedicated to engineering excellence, creativity, and personal growth. Our vision and mission reflect our commitment to these values as we move forward in our journey of engineering and innovation. Through our participation in competition of go-karts and electric vehicles, we put our engineering knowledge into practice. We take on real-world challenges, pushing the boundaries of what's possible in these fields.

MISSION

At Stallion Karting and Effique, we are on a mission to implement creative and innovative ideas in the realm of go-karts and electric vehicles. We actively participate in various competitions, where we design, build, and race these vehicles. Our dedication to innovation is our driving force. We constantly seek new and improved solutions in our projects.

VISION

We believe in the power of individual growth. We are committed to bringing out the best in each team member. By nurturing their talents and abilities, we create a diverse and skilled team that is ready to take on the challenges of engineering and innovation.
